"Korea's Winter Special: Suneung"

Article by Jihoon Song

The 2024 College Scholastic Ability Test (Suneung), which is considered the most important academic event in Korea, took place on November 16th, when the weather was unusually warm and clear. Even though test day has always been associated with cold temperature and sometimes first snow in winter, this year was different. Temperatures in Incheon and Seoul ranged from 10 degrees to as high as 15 degrees which is well above the average warm temperatures during the winter season with no snow across the peninsula.
