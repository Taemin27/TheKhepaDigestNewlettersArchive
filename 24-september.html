September Edition SPECIAL Recap: Chuseok

Known as Korea's Thanksgiving, Chuseok is what defines September in Korea.

 

One word that goes along with Chuseok is "Gratitude".

It has been a holiday to support the year's harvests, thanking the soil and the people around us for the bountiful harvest.

 

Similarly, this writing cycle's topic is not only exploring Chuseok cultures around Korea, but also expressing thanks to our environment, the people near us, and the accomplishments made this year in a certain field.
